PICY

PICY is an institution formed by people passionate about intercultural exchange. Since 1999 we have specialized in designing and facilitating cultural immersion programs in Yucatán, México. Through learning Spanish as a foreign language, we help our students to value cultural diversity in order to give them the capacity to interact and adapt in any part of the world.

The foundations of PICY’s work lies in our innovative methodology for language acquisition based 100% in cultural immersion, our support services for each student during their entire stay in Yucatán, and the flexibility of our programs to the needs of each individual or institution.

Our methodology in language acquisition has been perfected over fifteen years and is recognized by the Ministry of Education of Spain.
